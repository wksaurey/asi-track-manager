## ASI
* Autonomous Solutions Inc.
- The company at which I work
- We automate vehicles, generally heavy machinery for use in construction, ag, yard shifting, and mowing

## ASAM
* Autonomous Semi-Autonomous Machine
- an automated machine

## MMM
* Manned Monitored Machine
- A vehicle kitted with a gps and radio to communicate to Mobius its position for safety when in an AOZ with ASAMs

## Vehicle
* ASAM, MMM, or even general use vehicle outside of Mobius
- something like the Kubota that we use to transport safety riders to and from the tracks

## Mobius
* ASI's command and control software
- Tracks and controls ASAMs and MMMs to perform certain tasks
- Uses geo-fenced map shapes to control where vehicles can move and what they do while in that shape

## Tracks
* Test tracks at ASI where we test ASAMs and MMMs

## Safety Rider
* a rider in an ASAM who is prepared to stop autonomous operations

## AOZ 
* Autonomous Operating Zone
- Mobius map shape

## ADT
* Articulated Dump Truck
- Komatsu HM400-5

## WT
* Water Truck
- Komatsu HM400-5

## WL
* Wheel Loader
- Komatsu Wheel Loader

## Dozer
- Komatsu Dozer

## AG
* Agricultural Team at ASI
- Automates John Deere tractors for agricultural (and construction) use
